Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 18, 2026

Introduction

Rival ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the Rival mobile application.

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly, such as your username, profile picture, and fitness activity data. We also collect location data during active races to enable GPS-based competition features.

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to provide and improve the Rival experience, including matchmaking, leaderboards, race tracking, and social features. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

Location Data

Rival uses your location only during active races and with your explicit permission. Location data is used for real-time GPS race tracking and nearby rival discovery. You can disable location access at any time through your device settings.

Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption. We use Supabase for backend services, which provides enterprise-grade security and compliance.

Third-Party Services

We use Apple HealthKit and motion data (with your permission) to enhance race accuracy. We do not share HealthKit data with third parties or use it for advertising.

Data Deletion

You can request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us at support@myrivalapp.com. We will process deletion requests within 30 days.

Children's Privacy

Rival is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy within the app.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@myrivalapp.com.
